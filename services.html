Our Services

Everything you need to watch, create, and share premium video content.

Xstream provides a comprehensive video platform designed for creators and viewers alike. Below is a detailed overview of the services we offer, how they work, and what you can expect as a user of our platform.

Video Streaming & Playback

Watch content instantly in your browser

Our core service is high-quality video streaming directly in your web browser. No downloads or special software required. Features include:

  • Instant Playback — Videos begin playing immediately with adaptive buffering based on your connection speed.
  • HTML5 Player — Built-in video player with play, pause, volume, fullscreen, progress scrubbing, and picture-in-picture support.
  • Mobile Compatible — Fully responsive player that works on phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops.
  • No Ads — Clean viewing experience without pre-roll, mid-roll, or banner advertisements interrupting your content.
  • Multiple Formats — Supports MP4, WebM, MOV, AVI, MKV and more for maximum compatibility with uploaded content.

Search & Discovery

Find exactly what you're looking for

Our discovery system makes it easy to find content that matches your interests through multiple pathways:

  • Full-Text Search — Search through video titles, descriptions, and country tags to find specific content.
  • Category Filtering — Browse by category using chips, dropdown filters, or the sidebar category list.
  • Sorting Options — Sort videos by Most Recent, Trending (weighted score), Most Viewed, or Most Liked.
  • Trending Section — A dedicated homepage section highlighting videos with the highest engagement scores.
  • Related Videos — On every video page, see a sidebar of related content sorted by popularity.
  • Tag Cloud — Click trending tags in the sidebar to discover videos associated with popular topics.

Engagement & Interaction

React, share, and connect with content

We provide tools for viewers to engage with content and for creators to understand their audience:

  • Like / Dislike — Express your opinion on any video. Toggle on or off at any time. Only one reaction active per video.
  • View Counter — Every video tracks unique views. Anti-spam measures prevent artificial view inflation.
  • Share Links — One-click copy of video URLs to share on social media, messaging apps, or anywhere else.
  • Creator Analytics — View your total uploads, cumulative views, total likes, and total dislikes from your profile dashboard.
  • Persistent Preferences — Your like/dislike history is saved locally so your reactions persist across page reloads.

User Accounts & Profiles

Your identity on the platform

Creating a free account unlocks the full platform experience with personalized features:

  • Secure Authentication — Email/password sign-up and login powered by Firebase Auth with encryption.
  • Password Recovery — Forgot password flow with email-based reset links that expire for security.
  • Session Management — Choose "Remember Me" for persistent login or stay logged in for the current session only.
  • Profile Pages — Each user has a public profile showing their avatar, name, country, join date, and all uploaded videos.
  • Country Tagging — Your country is displayed on your profile and on videos you upload, helping viewers discover regional content.
  • Age Verification — Minimum age requirement of 13 years enforced at registration.

Safety & Support

Keeping the platform safe for everyone

We maintain a safe and trustworthy platform through dedicated safety infrastructure:

  • Content Reporting — Report inappropriate videos through our contact form. All reports are reviewed by our team.
  • Account Deletion — Request full account and data deletion through our support channel.
  • Help Center — Comprehensive FAQ with searchable articles covering all platform features and common issues.
  • Contact Support — Direct message form with subject categorization for faster response times.
  • Data Encryption — All authentication data is encrypted in transit and at rest via Firebase security standards.
  • Privacy Controls — Minimal data collection. See our Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy for full details.
